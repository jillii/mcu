Chart a new course for your banking needs with MCU. Open an account with no minimum deposit—from daily purchases to regular deposits and beyond, the Everyday Hero Account makes money management as easy as 1-2-3.

Features and Benefits:

  • End of the Minimum Balance Fee Era—Removal of $9 fee
  • Removal of the Debit Card Replacement fee—$5.00 fee
  • Removal of the NG/ Returned Check Fee (from other institutions) —$10
  • $0 minimum deposit to open and access to an MCU Debit Card
  • Deposit via Payroll Deduction or Direct Deposit
  • NYMCU® Digital Banking, including the Mobile App
  • Mobile Check Deposit
  • eStatements
  • MCU BillPay
